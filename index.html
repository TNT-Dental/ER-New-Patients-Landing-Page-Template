Dental Insurance Welcome

We realize the importance of taking care of your teeth, and we welcome dental insurance as a partner in making exceptional dentistry affordable. If you are fortunate enough to have PPO dental insurance, call us. We are an in-network provider for many of the leading plans, and our team will work with you to maximize your benefits.

Note: If you do not see your plan, do not worry. Call. We welcome other plans.

It All Starts with You

You will be amazed at the friendliness and thoroughness of all we do. With a passion for helping people smile, our team will provide you with a complete assessment of your teeth, gums and jaw. We will take all necessary x-rays and devote the time to answer all your questions.

If a dental emergency is the reason for your visit, our first priority is to get you out of pain.

If you are visiting for the first time as a new patient, you will receive a thorough evaluation of the health of your mouth. We will screen for oral cancer and take a tour of your teeth and gums. Also, we will evaluate the health of any existing crowns, inlays, onlays, dentures or partial dentures.
