Times have changed. Priorities have shifted. Today, nothing is more important than the health and safety of our staff and patients.

With this in mind, we have taken additional steps to make every visit to our office as safe as possible. Our goal is to add peace-of-mind to the friendliness and clinical excellence you have come to expect.

We will continue to make great dentistry affordable in a convenient, friendly and comfortable environment. And, we will continue to offer same-day appointments and flexible payment options. We are in this together, and we will never underestimate the power of a smile!

Dental Insurance Welcome

We realize the importance of taking care of your teeth, and we welcome dental insurance as a partner in making exceptional dentistry affordable. If you are fortunate enough to have PPO dental insurance, call us. We are an in-network partner with many of the leading plans, and our team will work with you to maximize your benefits.

Note: If you do not see your plan, do not worry. Call. We welcome other plans.

No Insurance? No Worries!

If you do not have dental insurance or would like to consider a better option, give us a call. We are proud to be the only practice in the area to offer our Be Smile Smart Plans. For a low, annual fee, individuals and families can receive their routine, preventive care along with significant discounts on other services. No limits. No deductibles. No surprises. In many cases, it is simpler, easier – and less expensive – than dental insurance.

It All Starts with You

You will be amazed at the friendliness and thoroughness of all we do. With a passion for helping people smile, our team will provide you with a complete assessment of your teeth, gums and jaw. We will take all necessary x-rays and devote the time to answer all your questions.

If a dental emergency is the reason for your visit, our first priority is to get you out of pain.

If you are visiting for the first time as a new patient, you will receive a thorough evaluation of the health of your mouth. We will screen for oral cancer and take a video tour of your teeth and gums. Also, we will evaluate the health of any existing crowns, inlays, onlays, dentures or partial dentures.
